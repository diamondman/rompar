to create template:

(note SHIFT will disable auto-centering)

columns:

left click on first bit in any row of any group

left click on last bit in any row of that group

left click on first bit in any row of each subsequent group

rows:

right click on any bit in first row of any group

right click on any bit in last row of that group

right click on any bit in each subsequent group

data/grid manipulation (after read command issued):

left click on any bit to toggle value

right click to select row

in manipulation mode:

left-arrow to move entire column left

right-arrow to move entire column right

up-arrow to move entire row up

down-arrow to move entire row down

DEL to delete row

BS to delete column
